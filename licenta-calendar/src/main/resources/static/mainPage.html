Gain device independence

There are manny aps that offer similar services with ours, but manny of them are not that easy ti use, or are not compatible with your mobile. With CVS you can establish, vote, or cancel any appointment you want using the device that it is closest to you.

What We Do

User management

Every user has each own schedule, but most of the time his program depends of other users. So in order to create a more stable app, and avoid over schedule appointments, we created a user hierarchy, and the higher is your rank, the more important your vote gets.

Polls for every request

Every user usually is part of a bigger group of people. We take all of this users and make pools witch can vote, propose, or cancel some events from calendar. Using this pools we can make people calendars interact with each other, for a better communication.

Strong voting system

Our application uses one of the most reliable algorithms for voting. Some of them are known as Borda's Rule, Fishburn's Rule, Plurality score and manny other.
